# Welcome to Cursuri AI 👋

**[Cursuri AI](https://cursuri-ai.ro)** is Romania's premium educational platform dedicated to professionals who want to master Artificial Intelligence and integrate it into production-grade architectures.

We bridge the gap between theoretical AI concepts and real-world business applicability, focusing on high-performance, scalable solutions.

## 🚀 Our Mission
To empower software engineers, data specialists, and business leaders with the practical skills required to build, optimize, and scale AI-driven workflows and applications. 

## 💻 The IT Pro Track
Our technical curriculum is built for developers who demand rigor. We focus on:
- Integrating LLMs and AI APIs into complex architectures.
- Writing production-ready, highly optimized code.
- Building scalable, stateless applications capable of handling high concurrency.
- Advanced prompt engineering and automated workflows.

## 🛠️ Our Tech Stack
The platform itself is built with a focus on enterprise-grade scalability and maintainability:
- **Backend:** PHP 8.5, Symfony 7.4, Doctrine ORM
- **Frontend:** React 19, TypeScript, Twig
- **Infrastructure:** MariaDB, Redis, Amazon SQS, Docker
